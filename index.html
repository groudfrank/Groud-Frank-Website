Business Operations Assistant I (Human Resources Department)

The unofficial title for this post is IHRIS clerk. IHRIS stands for Integrated Human Resources Information System. It is a digital database for the work records of public servants/civil servants in the republic of Trinidad and Tobago. I was responsible for updating and/or entering data related to pay increase, increments, leave taken(vacation, sick maternity,etc), leave granted, performance appraisals, records transfer either within the department or externally, e.g. Ministry of Education, etc.

Why Your Online Portfolio Matters?

When you're trying to market yourself to employers or clients visibility matters. You want to give them information about yourself that is easy to access especially in a competitive job market. Having an online portfolio is not meant to replace a cover letter or hard copy resume, instead it should enhance your suite of tools as a job or client seeker. It has your personal aesthestic touch(unlike a LinkedIn profile), it shows you are serious about promoting yourself and it allows you to track your progress as a professional.

Why a website matters?

Having a Website for your business or brand sends the message that you are serious about your brand. If your brand is soley dependent on a Faecbook page then it is at the mercy of Facebook and its interests. Your website means you control it. Your social media should complement your website, not the other way around. Whether it's built from scratch or with services like WordPress or Wix, let Groud do the technical lifting so you don't have to.

Why social media matters?

Reachability. You need to be as visible as possible. Social Media gives you access to an audiance numbering in the billions but there are so many options and not all of them are easy to use. Let Groud manage, update and post your thoughts, plans and products to that audiance. Think of him as Alfred from Batman or Geoffrey from Fresh Prince for your social media needs - your social media butler.

Why UI, UX and design matters?

UI means User Interface - It is how your website looks and feels. UX means User Experience - It is about how useable your website is. The first refers to form while the latter refers to function. What good is a website if it is visually unappealing? How useful would it be if it's difficult to nagivate or find information? Good UI and UX provides a pleasant experience for the user which is what you always want.

Why Open Source Matters?

Open Source AKA FOSS(Free Open Source Software) is a software model build on the idea of free(think free beer and freedom). FOSS licenses affords the ability to leverage and use the hard work of individuals or communities to make Groud's work easier and faster without the threat of legal action. Open Source is the future.
